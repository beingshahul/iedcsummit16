About IEDC

IEDC is the short for Innovation and Entrepreneurship Development Centre and are established in almost 147 technical institutions across the state. The Government of Kerala has created the “Startup Boot camp” Program wherein it is envisaged to create a startup ecosystem in each and every college campus through active involvement of students. Boot camp a.k.a IEDC is a student-focused program that will be completely run by students.The main objective is to foster entrepreneurship, develop and encourage a spirit of innovation and a drive to follow their passion amongst the student community. Thus through this programme Government aims to find out potential IEDCs across the state and provide them necessary support in order to enhance the entrepreneurial and innovation culture in the college including funding and mentoring support.
